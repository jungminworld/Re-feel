Re:feel's Brand Identity

Re:feel is...


Inspired by the lessons, comfort, and relaxation
gained from backpacking experiences in Vietnam and Thailand,
Re:feel reinterprets the warmth and sentiment of Southeast Asia's tropical climate.
Based on this, we present a global brand that offers tropical fruit cocktail and juices.

Our Value

We promise to provide
healthy food and drinks
with fresh ingredients everyday.

We always do our best
to keep your inner peace
with pleasant experiences.

We connect people and community.
Monthly activities are always
open to everyone.

We get fresh fruits by fair trade.
We keep eco-friendly campaign.
5% of profits are donated.

Our tropical cafe bar "Re:feel" is...


a sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation.
Just as meditation calms the mind and nourishes the soul,
our brand aims to provide an oasis of daily life.
Sip on our refreshing drinks, unwind in our vibrant atmosphere,
and immerse yourself in the moment.

Bridging World


At Re:feel, we believe in the power of globalization
to connect diverse cultures while also supporting
local traditions. Our mission is to serve as a bridge between worlds,
offering a platform for global exchange while celebrating
and preserving the rich cultural heritage of the local communities.
Join us as we embark on a journey to embrace diversity
and foster cultural understanding, one step at a time.


Cherish Your Relationships


At Re:feel, we empower you to cherish moments
with your loved ones—partners, friends, family, and more.
Enjoy our drinks and food, create unforgettable memories
and experiences to strengthen your bonds.
Join us at Re:feel and make the most of your time
with your people now!

More Than A Bar


We're more than just a bar -
we're a hub of new relationships.
With diverse activities and events, who knows?
You might just meet your soulmate or make lifelong friends!
Join us and connect with others in unforgettable ways.

Boost Your Energy


Get a boost of energy with our tropical fruit cocktail/juice
and feel revitalized to seize the day!
Recharge your life marathon and keep pushing forward,
even when you're feeling worn out.
